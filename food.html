About Us


Foodpanda is an online food delivery service that connects customers with a wide range of restaurants and food outlets. Founded in 2012, Foodpanda has rapidly expanded its presence across multiple countries, becoming one of the leading food delivery platforms globally. The platform allows users to browse through various cuisines, place orders, and have their favorite meals delivered straight to their doorstep. With a user-friendly interface and a vast network of partner restaurants, Foodpanda aims to provide a convenient and efficient food delivery experience for its customers.


Our Team


Our team is a diverse group of individuals who are passionate about food and technology. We come from different backgrounds and bring a variety of skills and experiences to the table. Our team is dedicated to providing the best possible service to our customers and partners, and we are constantly striving to improve and innovate. We believe in teamwork, collaboration, and open communication, and we work together to achieve our goals and make a positive impact in the food delivery industry.
